About Us

SpaceX, founded in 2002 by Elon Musk, is a private aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company with a vision to make life multiplanetary. SpaceX has redefined the possibilities of space exploration through cutting-edge technology, revolutionary rocket designs, and ambitious goals.

Our Mission

SpaceX aims to revolutionize space technology, enabling humanity to live on other planets. From reducing the cost of space travel to colonizing Mars, our mission is to ensure the survival of humanity by achieving sustainable space exploration.

History & Milestones

Since its founding, SpaceX has achieved remarkable milestones:

  • 2008: First privately-funded liquid-fueled rocket (Falcon 1) to reach orbit.
  • 2012: First privately-owned spacecraft (Dragon) to dock with the International Space Station (ISS).
  • 2015: First orbital rocket (Falcon 9) to land back on Earth for reuse.
  • 2018: First launch of Falcon Heavy, the most powerful rocket in operation.
  • 2020: First commercial crewed mission (Crew Dragon) to the ISS.
