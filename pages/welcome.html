Welcome

SpaceX was founded with a singular vision: to revolutionize space technology and make space exploration accessible for humanity. At its core, SpaceX's mission is to enable the colonization of Mars, reduce the cost of space travel, and pave the way for a multi-planetary future.

Since its inception, SpaceX has led the charge in aerospace innovation by designing and launching groundbreaking rockets, such as the Falcon 1, Falcon 9, and the world’s most powerful operational rocket, the Falcon Heavy. SpaceX also operates the Starlink project, a global satellite internet network that aims to connect the entire planet.

Join us as we embark on this journey to the stars, explore new frontiers, and make life multiplanetary. Whether it’s launching satellites, sending astronauts to the International Space Station, or building the technology to settle Mars—our work is only just beginning.

Begin your journey with SpaceX today and become part of humanity’s future.
